Overview

Xolair is a biologic treatment for servere asthma. It is an option for people ages 6+ who have servere asthma which cannot be mangaged by regular corticosteroids (drugs that lower inflammation and reduce immune system activity).

Visit Xolair's official website for more information

Genentech first released Xolair in 2003. They have since continued to find new uses for the thereapuetic in treating other diseases such as chronic idiopathic urticaria and nasal polyps. Xolair is also now available in prefilled syringes that patients can administer themselves at home. Previously Xolair had to be administered at a health care clinic. These syringes make Xolair more accessible to a wider range of patients.
